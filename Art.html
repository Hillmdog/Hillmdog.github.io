My Art Gallery

I always loved to draw and make arts and crafts growing up. as I got older, I started taking art electives in school and spent more time out of class working on my craft. In highschool, I took 4 years of art, with my last two being honors art. There was only one other person in the class. His name was Darrian. Becuase of this, we had alot of freeway in what we did for assiegments. Here are some of my pieces and a short Story on each.

Gallery

Lux

Lux was a piece I created my junior year. It was created for 100 neediest cases. It is a pen hatched piece with water color piece and white charcoal dusting. The missing piece is found on the lower left corner meant to represent the missing link we all need.

Unnamed

I drew this artwork my freshman year at UW. I was studying at Fetzer and wanted to take a study break. There is no meaning, more just a doodle.




Auxilium

Auxilium was one of my senoir projects. It is a full charcoal piece created for 100 needies cases. It was selected and published alongside a sotry. The piece is meant to represent how people are often walled off and seperated and must climb to be set free.

Chicago Skyline/Self Portrait

Both of these works are from my senoir year. The first is an abstract water color of the Chicago skyline. The second is a self portrait meant to work on color variation.

De Smet

This work was a collab with three other people. We created it for the De Smet auction and ended up selling for a little over 100$. It is a full acrylic poured piece with hand panted spartan helm and letters. The brim was poured in acrylic and is covered in a clear layer.

Pennywise

Pennywise was my final project my senoir year. I spent over 30 hours working on him and ended running out of time. He is a 3X4 foor charcoal portrait. I hope to one day finish him and display him in my house.
